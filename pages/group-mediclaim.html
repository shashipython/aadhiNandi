Overview

What Is a Group Mediclaim Plan?

Group Mediclaim is a health insurance plan offered to a group such as employees, members of an institution, or organized teams. It helps cover hospitalization and medical treatment expenses, depending on the plan structure and policy terms.

Medical Protection for Teams

Provides a health cover structure that supports employees or members during treatment and hospitalization.

Financial Relief During Emergencies

Helps reduce the burden of unexpected medical expenses for covered individuals and families.

Suitable for institutions looking for health protection under one organized policy structure.
