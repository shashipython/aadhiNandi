Overview

What Is Group Term Insurance?

Group Term Insurance is a life cover plan issued for a group such as employees, association members, borrowers, or institutional members. It helps provide financial support to beneficiaries in case of an insured member's death during the policy term.

Single Policy for Many Members

Organizations can cover multiple people under one structured protection plan.

Meaningful Employee Benefit

It adds real value to compensation and welfare packages for staff and teams.

Financial Support for Families

It helps beneficiaries with financial relief during difficult and uncertain times.

Simple and Practical Protection

Suitable for organizations that want broad protection with easy administration.
