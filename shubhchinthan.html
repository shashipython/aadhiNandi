📄 What is a Chit Fund?

A chit fund is a financial system in which a group of members come together and save money. In this system, all members contribute a fixed amount every month, and the total collected amount is given to one member each month through a process such as auction or selection.

In a chit fund, members can benefit both by saving and by borrowing. It is a scheme that helps individuals meet financial needs at the right time.

Chit funds are regulated under the Chit Funds Act, 1982, and are monitored and operated by the Central/State Government and Reserve Bank of India.
